# PaceBox ad video — Thai TVC comedy (the champion's demo)

- **Concept**: successful Thai TVC comedy formula — a world-champion racer archetype
  passionately explains to a deadpan Thai club rider how he overperforms thanks to
  the box + app, escalating into a full elbow-down pose on the garage floor.
- **Likeness caveat**: "Mark Markez" is a parody name that lives ONLY in the VO and
  end card (post-production). The generation describes a **fictional** Spanish
  MotoGP-legend archetype — using a real racer's likeness in a commercial without a
  license is a legal risk, and most models refuse recognizable celebrities anyway.
- **Platform**: Higgsfield · Seedance 2.0 · 9:16 · 8 s · single continuous shot ·
  3–4 takes, keep the best
- **Golden rules**: no text/logos/UI in the generation — brand, supers and the real
  app screen are composited in post.

## Prompt

```
Vertical 9:16, single continuous 8-second shot, photorealistic, Thai TV commercial
comedy style, bright pit-garage lighting, punchy saturated colors, slight film grain.

Inside a race pit garage at a Thai circuit, a sport bike with neon green accents
sits on a paddock stand. A world-champion motorcycle racer in full racing leathers —
a fictional charismatic Spanish MotoGP-legend archetype, helmet off, huge confident
grin — passionately explains his secret to a stunned young Thai club rider: he
points at a tiny matte-black telemetry box on the bike's tail, then at his
smartphone (screen angled away from camera), taps the rear tire with two fingers,
then suddenly drops into a full elbow-down cornering pose flat on the garage floor
to demonstrate lean angle, still pointing at the phone. The Thai rider, completely
deadpan, films everything on his own phone and nods furiously. Final half-second:
both give a thumbs-up to camera.

Escalating physical comedy: over-the-top champion energy against the rider's
deadpan, classic Thai TVC timing. Neon green and violet accent lighting in the
garage echoing a racing app interface. No text, no logos, no readable brand marks.
```

## Post-production

**VO (Thai with a Spanish accent — the farang-speaking-broken-Thai comedy staple):**

```
Markez: สี่สิบสามองศา! ยางหลังร้อนพอดี! กล่องนี้บอกหมดเลย!
Rider: โอ้โห… ซื้อที่ไหนครับ?!
```
*Markez: "43 degrees! Rear tire perfectly in temp! This box tells you everything!" —
Rider: "Whoa… where do I buy it?!"*

**Inserts and end card (never generated):**
- At t≈6 s: 1 s insert of the real LAP TIMER app screenshot (lap 9 green ★ with
  F96°/R99° — exactly what Markez is describing).
- End card: **"PaceBox — ขี่เร็วขึ้น เพราะรู้มากขึ้น"** (*"Ride faster, because you
  know more"*) + logo + QR.
- Sound: garage ambience + a distant screaming engine, music cut on the elbow-down
  pose, sting on the thumbs-up. Have the Thai lines proofread by a native speaker.
