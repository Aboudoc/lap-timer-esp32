# PaceBox video jingle — the brand sting

- **What it is**: the 8 s animated logo sting that closes every PaceBox video
  (ads, tutorials, social clips). One sound + one move + the mark locking dead
  center = the brand signature.
- **Asset choice**: use the COLOR lockup `assets/PaceBox_BRAND.jpg` as the final
  frame — the violet-P + green wordmark IS the brand and matches the app. The white
  sticker version (`PaceBox_STICKER_BW.jpg`) is the fallback if the sting must sit
  over footage instead of black.
- **How to keep the logo exact**: this is the ONE case where the logo enters the
  generation — as an **image input**, never as text in the prompt. On Higgsfield:
  Kling 3.0 with `PaceBox_BRAND.jpg` set as the **end frame** (best — pixel-exact
  landing), or Seedance 2.0 with the file attached as a reference image.
- **Format**: 16:9 master + a 9:16 re-run for Reels/TikTok end cards · 8 s
- **Audience note**: night pit lane = Thai riders' world (Buriram night vibes),
  no words needed — the engine does the talking.

## Prompt

```
8-second brand sting (logo jingle), 16:9, single continuous shot, photorealistic
with a clean graphic finish. IMAGE INPUT: the attached PaceBox lockup (violet
speed-P + green "PACEBOX" wordmark on black) is the FINAL FRAME — the mark must
land pixel-exact, perfectly centered.

Total darkness in a race-track pit lane at night. A single headlight flares far
away and grows fast. A supersport motorcycle blasts past the camera at full lean,
inches from the lens — its light wake smears into horizontal neon streaks of violet
and green across the pure black frame. The streaks decelerate, condense, and snap
into the speed-lines and letterforms of the logo, which ignites with a subtle neon
glow and locks dead center, pulsing once like a heartbeat. Background stays pure
black throughout.

High contrast, premium motorsport energy, subtle film grain. Camera dead-stable on
the final lock. No other text, no extra elements, no readable marks on the bike.
```

## Sound design (post-production — this is the actual "jingle")

- Inline-4 engine scream rising through the pass-by (doppler), then silence…
- …one dry **quickshifter pop** landing EXACTLY on the logo lock — that pop is the
  sonic logo, identical in every video.
- Optional low sub-bass pulse on the logo's single heartbeat.
