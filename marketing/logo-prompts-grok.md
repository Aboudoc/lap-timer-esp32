# PaceBox logo — Grok Imagine prompt

- **Goal**: one generation = a brand board of 5 distinct logo concepts for PaceBox,
  matched to the app's identity (near-black UI, neon green + violet accents).
- **Tip**: AI can garble short text — check the "PACEBOX" spelling on every
  proposal, then re-run the winning concept alone ("concept 3 only, refined, on a
  white and on a black background") to iterate.

## Prompt

```
Logo design sheet, 5 distinct logo concepts for "PaceBox", a motorcycle racing
telemetry device (lap timer, tire temperature, lean angle) with a companion app.
Presented as a clean brand board on a dark charcoal background, each concept shown
large with a small monochrome variant beside it.

Style: minimal flat vector, sharp geometry, motorsport energy, premium tech feel.
Palette: neon green and electric violet accents on near-black.

Concept directions:
1. A bold italic wordmark "PACEBOX" with a speed cut through the letters
2. A "P" monogram shaped like a racetrack corner with an apex point
3. A rounded-square box icon wrapped by a closed lap line forming a circuit loop
4. A minimalist stopwatch fused with a delta triangle
5. A lean-angle mark: a tilted motorcycle silhouette reduced to three lines at 43°

Crisp edges, high contrast, no photorealism, no gradients heavier than a subtle
neon glow, suitable as an app icon and as a sticker on a matte-black device.
```
