# Brand naming — the telemetry box

Shortlist generated 2026-07-23. **Chosen: PaceBox.**

## Candidates

**Short and literal (says what it does):**
- **PaceBox** ✅ chosen — clean, memorable, international
- LapSense / RaceSense — the box that "feels" the track
- TrackPulse — the pulse of your session

**Racing culture:**
- GhostLap — the "ghost" is the reference lap you chase; endless storytelling ("beat your ghost")
- ApexIQ — intelligence at the apex
- HoleShot — aggressive, speaks to riders
- Slyder — knee-slider nod, street-sounding

**Data / lean flavor:**
- LeanLab — fun, scientific
- Lean43 — 43° as the mythical angle (it's in the app screenshot)

## Avoid (trademarks)

- DeltaBox, Vmax — registered Yamaha marks.
